> [SAP Crystal Reports](https://www.sap.com/products/data-cloud/crystal-reports.html)
> is a business intelligence application used to design and generate reports from a wide range
> of data sources.

Each release is supported through two phases: **Mainstream Maintenance** (during which SAP delivers
support packages, patches, and fixes) and the **Priority One Support Phase** (covering only
high-priority issues without further patches). After both phases end, the version reaches end of life.

The same dates apply to the matching SAP Crystal Reports Viewer, SAP Crystal Server,
and SAP Crystal Reports Server (OEM edition) of each release year.

SAP has announced planned future releases: SAP Crystal Reports 2027 (Q1 2027, EOMM 2029-12-31)
and SAP Crystal Reports 2029 (Q1 2029, EOMM 2031-12-31).
