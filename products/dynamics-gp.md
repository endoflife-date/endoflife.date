> [Microsoft Dynamics GP](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Dynamics_GP) (formerly Great
> Plains) is an enterprise resource planning (ERP) product for small and medium-sized businesses.

In October 2019, Microsoft moved Dynamics GP (version 18.x) from the
[Fixed Lifecycle Policy](https://learn.microsoft.com/lifecycle/policies/fixed) to the
[Modern Lifecycle Policy](https://learn.microsoft.com/lifecycle/policies/modern).
Older releases (2013 through 2018 R2) remain on the Fixed Lifecycle Policy with five years of
Mainstream Support followed by five years of Extended Support.

On [2024-09-25, Microsoft announced the end of support for Dynamics GP](https://www.microsoft.com/dynamics-365/blog/it-professional/2024/09/25/announcing-end-of-support-for-dynamics-gp/).
Product enhancements, regulatory (tax) updates, and technical support end on **2029-12-31**
(the date was extended in January 2025 from the originally announced 2029-09-30).
Security updates remain available until **2031-04-30**.

After this date, Microsoft recommends migrating to
[Dynamics 365 Business Central](/dynamics-365-business-central).
