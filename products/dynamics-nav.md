> [Microsoft Dynamics NAV](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microsoft_Dynamics_NAV) (formerly Navision)
> is an enterprise resource planning (ERP) product from Microsoft, primarily aimed at small and
> medium-sized businesses. It was the direct predecessor of
> [Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central](/dynamics-365-business-central).

All Dynamics NAV versions follow the
[Fixed Lifecycle Policy](https://learn.microsoft.com/lifecycle/policies/fixed), which offers
five years of Mainstream Support followed by five years of Extended Support from the original
release date.

Starting in October 2018, Dynamics NAV was succeeded by Dynamics 365 Business Central on-premises.
Migration guidance is available on
[Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com/dynamics365/business-central/dev-itpro/administration/migrate-nav).
