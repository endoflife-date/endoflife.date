> [XenServer](https://www.xenserver.com/) is a Type 1 hypervisor based
> on the open-source Xen Project, used to run and manage virtual machines on
> physical servers.

XenServer was rebranded as Citrix Hypervisor in 2018 beginning with version 7.6,
 then renamed back to XenServer in 2023 when it became its own business unit
under Cloud Software Group.

With XenServer 8.4, the previous Current Release (CR) and Long Term
Service Release (LTSR) tracks were replaced by a single release stream.
Updates are delivered continuously through a content delivery network (CDN) and
applied to existing installations, rather than as discrete point releases.
XenServer 8.4 is supported until November 2028.

The preceding Citrix Hypervisor 8.2 line was a Long Term Service Release,
with its final Cumulative Update reaching end of life in June 2025
